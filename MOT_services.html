MOT Services

  • MOT service is charged at a flat rate of £40 , only if it's within 7 days before expiration.
  • An extra 30% is added if the MOT test is booked after the expiration date.
  • No MOT service is available before the 7-day window prior to expiration.
  • Vehicles may require a health check, service, or repair—charges vary by vehicle type and service needed.
  • Saturday bookings incur an additional 50% service charge and a flat £50 administrative fee.

* All prices are VAT exclusive.
